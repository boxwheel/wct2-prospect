2. **Available data inputs**:

   - `abecklas/WorldCupPlayers.csv`: 37,784 player-match rows with goal timing events ("G40'", "G87'"), positions, coach names

- **Champions with group stage loss**: 5 of 22 (22.7%) — West Germany 1954, West Germany 1974, Argentina 1978, Spain 2010, Argentina 2022
- **Extra time / penalty matches**: 73 of 964 (7.6%) — dates available to compute rest-day impact on next match
